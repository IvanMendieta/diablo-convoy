Who We Are

Diablo Convoy was created in 2018 to bring a full service workforce solution to the commercial transportation industry. Our team provides safe and reliable commercial truck drivers to private fleets, trucking companies and third-party logistics companies across California.

Wait, we got more...

Our goal is to provide a 5 service to our client partners and employees with a focus on building successful long term relationships.

Diablo Convoy was created by two long-tenured staffing professionals to partner with the Trucking, Logistics and Warehouse industry. Trucking is the life blood of e-Commerce today.

Diablo Convoy helps these industries eliminate the costs and time associated with recruiting, onboarding, HR and Compliance.

Services

This our a few of our work services, that we love to do here at Diablo Convoy.

Contract/Contract-to-Hire/Permanent Placement

We take care of all the pain points and details of the hiring process while you focus on your business operations

We handle all the sourcing, screening and recruiting of all candidates

We are laser focused on finding and meeting (In-person) the most qualified candidates for your positions while conducting a seamless Onboarding pro

DC Safety and Compliance

We work hand-in-hand with our clients to conduct site evaluations and safety audits per request

On a quarterly basis we conduct ride-a-longs with our drivers to ensure they are using best driver practices

DC consistently monitors industry trends to maintain our "5 Star" service to our drivers and clients

We keep a close eye on scoring data with our customers to ensure our drivers continue to meet DOT requirements.

DC offers online safety training to new drivers addressing close quarter maneuvers, pretrip inspections, avoidance procedures and accident reporting

Accident and Injury Prevention

We have a 5 point check system to ensure drivers are properly qualified under DOT regulations and meet all requirements

We partner with our clients in collecting all the information possible for a full accident and injury report

At Diablo Convoy we don't just focus on the accident we dive into the details on what could have been done to prevent it from occurring
